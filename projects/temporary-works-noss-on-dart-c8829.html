My role

Designed the king post retaining wall for the eastern boundary of Noss on Dart Marina. The calculation package covers the full temporary retaining wall design, including earth pressure analysis, king post sizing, and walings design.

Scope / key checks

  • Earth pressure analysis for the temporary retaining condition.
  • King post sizing and structural adequacy checks.
  • Walings design and connection details for the retained boundary.

Deliverables

  • One temporary works calculation pack under project reference C8829.
  • Full king post retaining wall design for the eastern boundary.
  • Preview pages and direct access to the complete document.
